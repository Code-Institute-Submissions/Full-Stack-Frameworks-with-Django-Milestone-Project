Hardware Requirements

The minimum hardware requirements for Extremewall V1.2 are:

  • 1 GHZ CPU (Intel or AMD)
  • 4 GB of RAM
  • 8GB disk drive (More is better for log files)
  • 2 Network Cards (Wireless and USB supported)
  • Installation Medium (USB or CD)
